Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

Summary: The CSV converter does not store, sell, or share any data. Your file is processed in server memory and discarded immediately after the PDF is returned to your browser.

What the app does

When you upload a CSV file, it is sent from your browser to the server, parsed in memory, and a colour-coded PDF report is generated and returned to your browser. No data is retained after the response is sent.

Data handling

  • CSV files are held in server memory only for the duration of processing, then discarded. They are never written to disk, never retained between requests, and never transmitted to any third party. The CSV may contain work-related data (task names, technician names, locations, dates) — none of this is logged or stored.
  • Report title is sent as a form field with each request. It is not stored on the server.

Network activity

The server makes no outbound network requests. All processing uses locally-installed libraries (pandas, ReportLab) — there are no external APIs and no third-party services involved in generating the PDF.

Third parties

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or any third-party data-collection services.

Data sale and transfer

No user data is sold or transferred to any third party, used for any purpose unrelated to generating your PDF, or used to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.
